FAQ

Where is The Short Hall in Vienna?

The Short Hall is at Burggasse 17, 1070 Wien, in Vienna’s 7th district.

Does The Short Hall show live sports?

Yes. The Short Hall describes itself as a sports pub showing football, rugby, Champions League nights, major tournaments and international sport across multiple screens.

Does The Short Hall have any confirmed deal?

Yes. The official website lists Monday Stelze Night: beer roasted ham hock with trimmings and a pint of Kozel for €25, available every Monday.

What are The Short Hall opening hours?

The official opening hours are Sunday to Thursday from 12:00 to 01:00, and Friday to Saturday from 12:00 to 02:00.

Is The Short Hall the same as The Long Hall?

No. The Short Hall is a separate venue in the 7th district, created as a smaller sister pub inspired by The Long Hall.
