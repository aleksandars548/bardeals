FAQ

Where is Charlie P’s in Vienna?

Charlie P’s Irish Pub is at Währinger Straße 3, 1090 Wien, in Vienna’s 9th district.

Does Charlie P's show live sports?

Yes. Charlie P's describes itself as one of Vienna's premier sports bars and mentions 8 big TVs, including Champions League, Premier League, Serie A, Bundesliga, La Liga, rugby and NFL.

Does Charlie P's have happy hour?

The official website mentions cellar bar happy hour from Tuesday to Saturday, 19:00-20:00. Check the current event details with the venue before ordering.

What are Charlie P's opening hours?

Official pub hours are Monday 15:00-01:00, Tuesday to Thursday 15:00-01:30, Friday 15:00-03:00, Saturday 12:30-03:00 and Sunday 12:30-01:00.

Is Charlie P's good for World Cup or Champions League games?

Yes. Charlie P's publishes sports schedules and promotes major live football, including Champions League and selected FIFA World Cup 2026 matches.
