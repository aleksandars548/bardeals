FAQ

Are all deals confirmed?

Confirmed offers are shown when a venue publishes them or the information was already checked. If a deal is not fully confirmed, the page tells you to check directly with the venue.

Why do most cards use placeholder images?

BarDeals only uses owned, supplied or legal placeholder images. We do not copy Google Maps guest photos or random image-search photos.

How should I use the Google Maps buttons?

Open the venue in Google Maps, check directions, current opening times and recent venue updates before visiting.

Can a venue get a full bar page?

Yes. Full pages can include real photos, official offers, events and richer venue details once the information is confirmed.

Deals, prices, opening times and events can change. Always check directly with the venue before visiting.
