Quick Answer: Where to Find Happy Hour in Vienna

The easiest way to find happy hour in Vienna is to look around the city centre, Wieden, Neubau, Schwedenplatz, Westbahnhof and the Irish pub scene. The best choice depends on what you want: cocktails, cheap beer, shots, pub food, sports, rooftop drinks or a late-night bar.

For cocktail deals, start with Santos Wieden and Hard Rock Cafe Vienna. For shots and party drinks, check Daiquiri Shotothek. For pub atmosphere, try Charlie P's, Belushi's Vienna, The Long Hall or The Short Hall. For a bigger sports night, check MEINZ Sportsbar or ADMIRAL Arena Prater.

Before you go

Happy hour prices and times can change. Always check the venue's latest menu, Instagram, website or staff before ordering. This guide is designed to help you choose quickly, not to replace the venue's current menu.

What is the best happy hour in Vienna?

For cocktails, Santos Wieden and Hard Rock Cafe Vienna are strong choices. For shots, check Daiquiri Shotothek. For pub atmosphere, Charlie P's and Belushi's Vienna are good options.

Are happy hour prices always updated?

Which bars in Vienna have cocktail deals?

Santos Wieden, Hard Rock Cafe Vienna, Daiquiri Shotothek and Matiki Bar are good places to check if you are looking for cocktails or mixed-drink offers.
